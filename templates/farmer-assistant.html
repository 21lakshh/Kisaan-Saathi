What are the best practices for growing tomatoes in a small garden?

Here are some best practices for growing tomatoes in a small garden:

  • Choose determinate varieties for small spaces
  • Use vertical supports to maximize space
  • Plant in well-draining soil with organic matter
  • Water consistently at the base, not the leaves
  • Apply balanced fertilizer (5-10-10) after first fruit set
